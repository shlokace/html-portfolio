The Best Movies according to Shlok.

My top 3 movies of all time.

Ramayan (Animated by director Yugo Saki)

This taught many of the people of my generation the beauty of Indian history.
Absolutely marvelous piece of cinematography and that too by a foreign director.

Sholay

Absolute classic of Bollywood. Still reigns as one of the best pictures Bollywood has ever produced.

Kashmir Files

Taught the Indian population about the genocide of Hindus that took place in the Indian state of Jammu&Kashmir.
